2. **Lien priority among secured tranches** — the three secured notes (7.88% '27, 4.00% '28, 7.00% '29) are all "first-priority," but their relative ranking and intercreditor terms are not disclosed → indenture / intercreditor / collateral (security) agreements.
3. **Call / redemption schedules & make-whole formulas** — none disclosed for any instrument → individual note indentures (exhibit-indexed).
4. **Convertible Notes terms** — conversion price/ratio, fundamental-change put, and the maturity-label discrepancy ("Dec 2025" in the table vs "2027 Convertible Notes" in the narrative) → convertible note indenture / prior 10-K.
5. **Full covenant definitions** — how "adjusted EBITDA," "debt to capital," "consolidated reserves," and "liquidity" are defined → credit / facility / export-credit agreements.
6. **Per-instrument guarantor lists** — guarantors are "substantially the same entities"; the specific list, and how the $1.8B Sun Princess export facilities map to particular table lines, are not given → indentures / guarantee agreements.
7. **Currency labeling** — only EUR lines are explicitly denominated; confirm the non-EUR lines are USD → prior 10-K / prospectuses.
8. **Instrument identifiers / issue economics** — CUSIP/ISIN, issue dates, original issue discount, and pricing are not in the schedule (only the cover-page registered security "1.000% Senior Notes due 2029," CUK29, is named) → prospectus supplements / indentures.
9. **FX vs repayment split** on EUR-denominated balance movements — not separately disclosed per line → derivatives footnote / 10-K.
10. **Convertible settlement reconciliation** — how the $1,131M carrying reduction maps across the $500M cash payment, $618M to equity, and the $945M aggregate repayments line is not reconciled in the filing.
11. **Cross-reference to the 2025 Form 10-K** — the 10-Q directs readers to the "2025 joint Annual Report on Form 10-K" for full detail → pull the 10-K for complete debt terms.
